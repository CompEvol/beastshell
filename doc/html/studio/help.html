BEAST Studio help

This panel shows online documentation and help.

To navigate through the history, click the next/previous buttons in the toolbar. Click the home-icon to go back to the BEASTStudio help screen.

To search for text in a page, go to the text field in the toolbar, and click enter to start searching. Ctrl-F will bring you there as well. Search forward or backward with the seach-down/up buttons in the toolbar.

Right click to show a menu for browsing back/forward or reloading a page.

Short-cut keys:

Ctrl-Fstart searching
Alt-rightgo forward one page
Alt-leftgo back one page
+zoom in
-zoom out
tabgo to next link
Shift-tabgo to previous link
Enterselect current link
Left/Right/Up/Down/Page Up/Page DownScroll the page
